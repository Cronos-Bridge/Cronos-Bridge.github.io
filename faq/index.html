What is Cronos Bridge?

Cronos Bridge is a cross-chain liquidity bridge built natively on Cronos Chain, enabling users to bridge tokens with low fees, zero slippage, and full custody of their assets.

Which digital assets are supported on Cronos Bridge?

Cronos Bridge supports a wide variety of tokens including major assets such as BTC, ETH, SOL, AVAX, and LINK, which can be traded instantly inside layer-2 pools.

Is Cronos Bridge audited and secure?

Yes, Cronos Bridge smart contracts are audited by top-tier Web3 security firms. The protocol uses non-custodial smart contracts, meaning users retain full ownership of their credentials.

How does Cronos Bridge reduce transaction gas fees?

Cronos Bridge utilizes zk-rollup batching technology on Cronos Chain. This technology bundles transactions together to significantly decrease the gas fees compared to traditional Ethereum trades.

Gas Optimizations and Network Speed
