What is Cronos Bridge?

Cronos Bridge is the official Cronos EVM Bridge web app for moving supported assets between Cronos POS, Cronos EVM, and selected Cosmos IBC networks; Cronos docs describe it as a decentralized protocol built on open-source IBC infrastructure.

How Cronos Bridge works

  1. Connect walletOpen the official app, connect a supported wallet, and approve the prompt in the wallet interface.
  2. Choose routeSelect the origin network, destination network, and asset; verify the active wallet network and recipient address.
  3. Enter amountReview the amount, displayed network fees, and destination details before continuing.
  4. Confirm transferSign the wallet request, then track the bridge status in the app history or linked explorer view.

Cronos Bridge FAQ

What is cronos bridge used for?

It is used to move supported assets between Cronos POS, Cronos EVM, and selected Cosmos IBC networks through the official Cronos web app.

Is cronos bridge the official app?

The official route listed by Cronos documentation is https://cronos.org/bridge; always verify the domain before connecting a wallet.

Which wallets work with cronos bridge?

Cronos documentation references support for wallet flows including MetaMask, Keplr, Crypto.com Onchain Wallet, and Rabby, depending on route and environment.

Does cronos bridge charge fees?

The official FAQ says the bridge service itself does not add a service fee, but network gas fees still apply.

How long does cronos bridge take?

Cronos states that IBC transfers can take from about 1 minute to 1 hour depending on congestion, with unresolved transfers expected to complete or revert.
